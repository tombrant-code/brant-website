Who We Are

Straightforward Financial Guidance, Down to the Bottom Line.

Brant Professional Services is a New York-based financial consulting firm founded in 2021. We provide CFO services, tax and accounting, capital advisory, and operational consulting to small and mid-sized businesses, nonprofits, high-net-worth individuals, and entrepreneurs across a wide range of industries.

We started this firm because too many clients were getting financial and accounting advice buried under corporate jargon, slow processes, and unnecessary complexity. Our goal is to take the opposite approach: transparent, practical guidance that’s easy to understand and delivers simple solutions.

We also believe in looking at things from approaches other firms overlook. Some of the most valuable insights come from asking unconventional questions or considering possibilities that are too often overlooked — the kind that surface real savings, real liability, or real opportunity that might otherwise go unnoticed.

And when you call us, you reach a person — not an automated menu. No hold music, no button-pressing to navigate to the right department. Our clients talk directly to us, every time.

Our Approach

We Work Like an Insider.

When we’re engaged, we get to know the business the way someone on the inside would — not just the numbers, but also the relationships, the pressures, and the decisions that don’t show up on a balance sheet. This perspective serves as the foundation for our recommendations, rather than applying a generic framework.

In practice, that means building budgeting and forecasting models that actually reflect how the business runs, reviewing the numbers before the board does, and catching the tax exposure before it becomes a problem. The goal is to make sure financial decisions are made with the full picture in mind.

A merchant cash advance (MCA) is an advance against your future receivables, repaid as a fixed percentage of daily sales. There is no set term — payback accelerates when revenue is high and slows when it is low. A term loan is a fixed lump sum repaid on a set schedule with a defined interest rate. MCAs are faster and more accessible for businesses with limited credit history; term loans typically carry lower effective costs for businesses that qualify. We help you compare both side by side before you commit to anything.

What We Offer

Financing products

Different goals call for different structures. We help you match the right product to your situation and source it through our lender network.

Revenue-Based Financing
Funding repaid as a small share of sales — flexible for businesses with steady deposits.
Business Term Loans
A fixed amount repaid over a set schedule — predictable for planned investments.
Business Lines of Credit
Draw what you need, when you need it — useful for managing cash-flow gaps.
Equipment Financing
Acquire vehicles, machinery, or tools while preserving working capital.
Invoice Factoring & A/R Financing
Turn outstanding invoices into cash today instead of waiting on net terms.
Bridge Loans
Short-term financing to cover the gap while longer-term funding or a sale comes together.
Fix & Flip / Rehab Loans
Short-term financing to purchase, renovate, and resell investment properties.
Consolidation Loans
Refinance and combine existing balances into a single, more manageable payment.
SBA Loans
Government-backed financing with competitive terms for qualified small businesses. Timelines vary by program and can take several weeks.
